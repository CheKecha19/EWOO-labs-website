Roadmap v1.0
EWOO's timeline
1
Phase 1: Q3-Q4 2022 // Solana expansion
Phase 1 is aimed at building trust between us as a project and the community as future users. While we are looking for projects ready for cooperation, our developers will make every effort to release a product of the highest quality in the shortest possible time. Soon after the launch, we plan to release the first games and stake our NFT, which will benefit not only players, but also those who just believe in our project.

  • Team assembling
  • Mint testing
  • Website launch
  • Public mint & secondary market listings
  • DAO launch
  • Paid Solana beta-test
  • 1st 3 games on Solana
2
Phase 2: Q4-Q1 2022-2023 // NEAR expansion
After we have completed the tasks of establishing trust with players and releasing the first three games, we will expand our activities to the NEAR blockchain. There we plan to get some fame thanks to the release of the NFT collection. Shortly before the release of the collection, we plan to release the first game on this blockchain and increase the number of games released on the Solana blockchain to 6.

  • Increase number of games to 6
  • NEAR collection
3
Phase 3: Q1-Q2 2023 // Ethereum expansion
After the release of the first three games on the NEAR blockchain, we plan to expand our influence on the Ethereum blockchain. We also plan to gain fame in this blockchain by releasing a collection on the Ethereum network. At the same time, we plan to re-issue our token and conduct an IDO token, having received support from large investors and funds, enter the world market. We will also release the remaining planned games on the previous two blockchains

  • Increase games modes to 15
  • Eth collection
  • Token IDO
